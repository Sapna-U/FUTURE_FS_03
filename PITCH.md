# Pitch — Southern Brew Website

**To:** Southern Brew (café, Erode)
**From:** Sapna U
**Re:** A new website for the café

## The problem

Southern Brew doesn't have a website today. Anyone searching for it online only finds it
through word of mouth or by walking past — there's no way to check the menu, hours, or
location before visiting, and no easy way for happy customers to share it with others.

## What this website does

- **Menu, always up to date** — customers can check what's available and the price before
  they arrive, instead of calling to ask.
- **Hours and location up front** — fewer "are you open right now?" calls, and a direct
  "Get Directions" link removes the friction of finding the place the first time.
- **Reviews built in** — social proof for anyone discovering the café for the first time
  online.
- **Mobile-first** — most people will find this on their phone while deciding where to
  get coffee nearby, so the site is designed to work well on a small screen.
- **Shareable** — a proper web presence makes it easy to link from Instagram/Google
  listings, and easy for customers to forward to friends.

## Why it helps the business grow

1. **More first-time visitors** — people searching "filter coffee near me" or "café
   Erode" have something to find and click into.
2. **Fewer repeat questions** — hours, menu, and location are answered instantly,
   saving staff time on the phone.
3. **Stronger first impression** — a clean, well-presented site signals quality before
   the customer has even walked in.
4. **Foundation for growth** — once this is live, it's straightforward to add online
   ordering, a loyalty section, or event announcements later without starting over.

## Tech used

Built with Next.js and Tailwind CSS, deployed as a static site (works on any free
hosting — Vercel, Netlify, or even a shared WordPress host if preferred later).
